Privacy Policy

This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We aim to be transparent, minimize data collection, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect only the data needed to provide and improve our services: contact information you submit, usage analytics to understand and improve site performance, and any details you share when interacting with our team. We avoid collecting unnecessary personal data.

How We Use Information

Information is used to respond to inquiries, deliver services, personalize experiences, and comply with legal obligations. Aggregated analytics help us make the site faster and more useful. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Security & Retention

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect data. Retention periods are limited to what is necessary for the purposes described and as required by law. If you have concerns about data retention, contact us.

Your Rights

You can request access to the personal data we hold about you, ask for corrections, or request deletion where applicable. To exercise these rights, use the contact options below and include sufficient detail so we can verify your request.
